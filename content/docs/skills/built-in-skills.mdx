NevoFlux ships with the following skills. Invoke any of them with `/<name>`.

## Knowledge base

### brain
The hub for your [knowledge base](/en/docs/knowledge-base). It orients you and routes to
the four focused brain skills below, loading shared conventions. Triggers on phrases
like "my knowledge base" / "second brain" / "知识库".

```text
/brain
```

### brain-recall
**Read and recall** from your knowledge base — search and query pages, recall facts,
see what's notable or recent, follow links and timelines. Read-only.

```text
/brain-recall what do I know about project Acme?
```

### brain-capture
**Save and ingest** into your knowledge base — file pages, update the distilled
"truth", append a dated timeline entry, tag and link. Can ingest PDFs/office docs,
video subtitles, and images.

```text
/brain-capture save this article to my brain
```

### brain-think
**Synthesize and analyze** across your knowledge base — multi-hop, cited answers;
"who knows about X"; trends and trajectories; graph traversal.

```text
/brain-think connect the dots between my notes on pricing and churn
```

### brain-care
**Maintain** your knowledge base — health and doctor reports, stats, find
contradictions/orphans (report only), sync the index, restore soft-deletes, revert a
bad edit. Never rewrites or deletes on its own.

```text
/brain-care is my brain healthy?
```

## Creation

### app
Build an interactive **[Canvas](/en/docs/canvas) app** — an HTML single-page app, a
React/Vue/Svelte multi-file project, an SVG, a Mermaid diagram, or Markdown — using
the NevoFlux SDK (storage, events, tools, sharing).

```text
/app a budget dashboard with a pie chart
```

### video
Author a video composition that renders to MP4. Great for short product intros,
motion graphics, and social formats (e.g. 9:16).

```text
/video a 9:16 product intro with our logo
```

## Workflow

### loop
Re-run a prompt (or a wrapped skill) on a recurring time/event trigger. Each
iteration starts fresh; small state persists in a scratchpad. Loops can also gate,
verify, and even propose improvements to themselves — see
[Automation](/en/docs/features/automation#loops-recurring-tasks).

```text
/loop 5m check this page for new comments
```

### schedule
Run a task on a calendar schedule — a cron cadence (minimum 1 hour) or a one-off
time — as a background job. See [Scheduled tasks](/en/docs/features/scheduling).

```text
/schedule every weekday at 9am, summarize my unread newsletters
```

### goal
Give the agent a completion condition for the current session; it re-evaluates after
every turn until the goal is met or its turn budget runs out. See
[Goals](/en/docs/features/scheduling#session-goals-goal).

```text
/goal keep fixing until `cargo test` exits 0
```

### skill-creator
Create, modify, and improve skills — including drafting a new `SKILL.md`, optimizing
a skill's description, and measuring its performance. See
[Authoring skills](/en/docs/skills/authoring).

```text
/skill-creator turn this workflow into a reusable skill
```
